# Customer-Churn-Analysis
This project anlayzes customer churn behaviour to understand why customers leave and how it impacts business revenue. The dashboard was built using Microsoft Excel with pivot tables, charts, interactive data summaries

## BUSINESS PROBLEMS
Customers churn leads to revenue loss and reduced customer lifetime value. This prokect aims to identify patterns and factors influencing churn

## DATASET
Total Customers: 15,000
Features include:
  * Contract Type
  * Payment method
  * Customer Tenure
  * Charges
  * Churn Status

## DASHBOARD METRICS
* Total Customers
* Churn Rate
* Revenue lost
* Total Charges

## KEY INSIGHTS
1. Nearly 1 out of every 3 customers leaves the service
2. The high rate of churn is causing significant financial loss
3. Monthly customers churn far more than yearly customers.
4. Credit card users shows the higghest churn numbers
5. Customers in the first 6-12 months are most likely to leave.

## TOOLS USED 
* Microsoft Excel
* Pivot Tables
* Data Visualization
* Data Cleaning

## BUSINESS RECOMMENDATIONS
* Encourage yearly contracts through discounts
* Improve onboarding for new customers
* Investigate payment isssues related to credit cards
